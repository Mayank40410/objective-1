Track 2.3: Text Cleaning in RAG Systems Detailed Guidelines Objective The purpose of Text Cleaning is to convert raw extracted text from PDFs, DOCX files, websites, or scanned documents into a clean and standardized format before chunking and embedding generation. A poorly cleaned document leads to: Irrelevant chunks Poor retrieval accuracy Increased hallucinations Wasted storage and computation Why Text Cleaning is Important Consider the following text extracted from a PDF: Research Paper on Artificial Intelligence 1. Introduction Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming industries. -- 1 of 18 -- Research Paper on Artificial Intelligence 2. Literature Review Problems: Page numbers Repeated headers Separator lines Extra blank spaces Formatting noise If stored directly in a vector database, retrieval quality decreases significantly. Text Cleaning Workflow Raw Document Text Extraction Remove Noise Normalize Text Validate Output Chunking -- 2 of 18 -- Task 1: Remove Extra Spaces Problem Artificial Intelligence is transforming industries. Solution import re text re.sub(r's', ' ', text) Output Artificial Intelligence is transforming industries. -- 3 of 18 -- Task 2: Remove Multiple Blank Lines Problem Introduction Artificial Intelligence Solution text re.sub(r'n', 'n', text) Output Introduction Artificial Intelligence -- 4 of 18 -- Task 3: Remove Page Numbers Problem Solution text re.sub(r'Pagesd', '', text) -- 5 of 18 -- Task 4: Remove Headers and Footers Problem Every page contains: XYZ University Research Report Solution text text.replace( "XYZ University Research Report", "" ) For dynamic detection: from collections import Counter Identify frequently repeated lines and remove them. -- 6 of 18 -- Task 5: Remove Special Characters Problem Artificial Intelligence Solution text re.sub( r'a-zA-Z0-9s.,!?;:()-', '', text ) Output Artificial Intelligence -- 7 of 18 -- Task 6: Fix Broken Sentences Problem PDF extraction often gives: Artificial Intelligence is transforming industries and creating new opportunities. Solution text text.replace("n", " ") Output Artificial Intelligence is transforming industries and creating new opportunities. -- 8 of 18 -- Task 7: Unicode Normalization Problem AI Machine Learning Different quotation marks may cause inconsistencies.
